This repository contains a list of bugs found on the Academy Bugs website. These bugs affect the user experience and need to be addressed.

## Bugs

### Bug #1: **Crash Bug - Page Becomes Unresponsive When Clicking on Number of Results**
- **Severity:** High
- **Issue Type:** Crash
- **Frequency:** Every Time

#### Action Performed:
1. Open [https://academybugs.com](https://academybugs.com).
2. Click the "Find Bugs" link in the navigation bar.
3. Click the buttons to show a certain number of results at the top left.

#### Expected Result:
- The selected number of results is displayed according to the clicked buttons.

#### Actual Result:
- The page freezes when clicking on the numbers of results.

### Bug #2: **Performance Bug - Product in Hot Item Section Keeps Loading**
- **Severity:** High
- **Issue Type:** Performance
- **Frequency:** Every Time

#### Action Performed:
1. Open [https://academybugs.com](https://academybugs.com).
2. Click the "Find Bugs" link in the navigation bar.
3. Open a product.
4. Open the product in the Hot Item section of the right side menu.

#### Expected Result:
- The product in the Hot Item section is displayed.

#### Actual Result:
- The page loads infinitely.

### Bug #3: **Content Bug - Unreadable Symbols in Shopping Cart Popup**
- **Severity:** Low
- **Issue Type:** Content
- **Frequency:** Every Time

#### Action Performed:
1. Open [https://academybugs.com](https://academybugs.com).
2. Click the "Find Bugs" link in the navigation bar.
3. Add one or more products to the cart.
4. Click "View Cart" on top of the page.
5. Scroll down to the Shopping Cart section of the right side menu.
6. Hover over the Shopping Cart caption.

#### Expected Result:
- All characters are clear and readable to a regular user.

#### Actual Result:
- There are unreadable symbols in the shopping cart popup.
